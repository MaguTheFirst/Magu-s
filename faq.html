Frequently Asked Questions

Your most common questions answered in one place.

How fast is delivery? ▼
Our automated system delivers Robux within 5-15 minutes once payment is confirmed. During peak hours, delivery can take up to 60 minutes, and you will see live status updates in your order tracker.
Is Rubox safe? ▼
Yes. We never ask for your Roblox password, we rely on secure group payout methods, and we enforce TLS 1.3 + HSTS on every request.
What payment methods are supported? ▼
We accept bank transfers, PayPal, credit/debit cards, and WhatsApp payments. Regional wallets (STC Pay, Mada) are supported for KSA shoppers.
What if my Robux doesn't arrive? ▼
Contact support immediately via WhatsApp or Discord and share your order ID. We'll verify the transaction and either push delivery again or refund you—no questions asked.
Do you offer bulk discounts? ▼
Yes! Orders above 10,000 Robux qualify for tiered pricing. Reach out through the contact page for a custom quote.
